Your First Step

We understand that starting therapy can be daunting. Here is what to expect in your initial consultation.

1

Matching

Fill out our brief matching questionnaire. We'll pair you with a therapist whose expertise aligns with your needs.

2

Discovery

A 50-minute session to share your story. Your therapist will listen, understand your goals, and begin forming a path forward.

3

Planning

Together, you'll establish a cadence and focus for future sessions that fits your schedule and emotional capacity.

Ready to take the next step?

The hardest part is starting. We're here to make the rest of the journey as gentle as possible. Your transformation begins with a single conversation.
