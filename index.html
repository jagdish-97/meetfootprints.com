Insurance & coverage

We believe cost shouldn't be a barrier to getting support.

At Footprints, we work with a wide range of insurance plans, including Medicaid, so that more people in our communities can access the care they deserve. If you're not sure whether your plan is accepted, we're happy to help you figure it out before your first appointment.

Plans we currently accept

New York

  • Emblem Health Medicaid / Enhanced Care
  • Excellus Medicaid (HMO & POS)
  • Wellcare Medicare
  • Ambetter Health
  • VNS Insurance (NY)
  • Aetna Medicare Assure Plan (HMO)

New Jersey

  • Aetna Better Health of NJ
  • NJ Alternative Benefit Plan (NJ Medicaid)
  • NJ FamilyCare / Amerigroup
  • Amerigroup (NJ Medicaid)
  • UHC Medicaid (NJ)
  • Wellpoint NJ Medicaid
  • Horizon Medicaid / Horizon NJ Health

Florida

  • Florida Medicaid / Sunshine Health
  • Simply Medicaid (FL Medicaid)

Other accepted plans

  • Tricare West

Don't see your insurance?

Insurance coverage is always evolving, and we may accept additional plans not listed here. Reach out to our team and we'll verify your benefits before your first session, no commitment needed.

Contact us to verify your insurance

We also offer a sliding scale fee for clients who are uninsured or underinsured. Access to mental health care matters. Talk to us and we'll do our best to find a path that works for you.
