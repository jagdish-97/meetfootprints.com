Rate Increase Notice

Self-pay session rate update effective June 11, 2026.

We are writing to inform you of an upcoming adjustment to our self-pay session rate at Footprints Mental Health Counseling, P.C.

Effective June 11, 2026, our out-of-pocket rate for therapy sessions will be updated from $95.00 to $150.00 per session.

Published

May 11, 2026

Who This Affects

Self-pay clients only. Insurance copay, coinsurance, and deductible amounts are not affected.

Important Details

What is changing and when.

The new self-pay rate of $150.00 per session applies to all appointments on or after June 11, 2026.

Sessions scheduled and occurring before June 11, 2026 will continue to be billed at the current rate of $95.00.

Rate change scope

This adjustment applies to self-pay therapy sessions.

Insurance clients

Your insurance-based costs are determined by your plan and are unchanged by this notice.

Effective date

All sessions on or after June 11, 2026 are billed at $150.00.

Before June 11, 2026

Sessions occurring before this date are billed at $95.00.

Need Financial Support?

Sliding scale support may be available.

If you are uninsured and have concerns about the new rate, you can request information about our sliding scale program.

Sliding scale rates are available exclusively to clients without insurance coverage and require a formal request and eligibility review.

To submit a request or speak with our team, call (917) 352-3625 or email intake@footprintstofeelbetter.com.
